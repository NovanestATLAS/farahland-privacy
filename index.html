Privacy Policy — FarahLand

Last updated: 21 July 2026

In one sentence: FarahLand does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data whatsoever. It works entirely offline on your device.

1. Who we are

FarahLand ("the app") is an educational game for children aged 2 to 5, published by the FarahLand developer ("we", "us"). This policy explains how the app handles information. Contact: quizzy.quest.planet@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

None. The app does not collect any personal or personally identifiable information. Specifically, it does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers or postal addresses
  • Photographs, audio or video recordings
  • Location data of any precision
  • Contacts, calendar entries or files on the device
  • Device identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Usage analytics, crash reports or behavioural data

3. Accounts and registration

No account, login, registration or sign-in of any kind is required or offered.

4. Internet access

The app functions entirely offline. It makes no network requests, contacts no server, and requires no internet connection to work.

5. Third parties

The app contains no third-party software development kits (SDKs), no analytics providers, no social network integrations and no advertising networks. No data is shared with anyone, because no data exists to share.

6. Advertising

The app contains no advertising of any kind: no banners, no interstitials, no rewarded video, no cross-promotion and no sponsored content. There is no personalised or interest-based advertising, and no remarketing.

7. In-app purchases

There are none. The app is free and contains no paid content.

8. Information stored on the device

The app keeps the child's coloring progress and the chosen interface language in the app's own memory so it can restore a drawing that was left unfinished. This information never leaves the device, is not readable by any other app, and is removed when the app is uninstalled.

9. Device permissions

The app requests no sensitive permissions. It does not access the camera, microphone, location, contacts, call logs, SMS or external storage.

10. Children's privacy

FarahLand is designed specifically for children. It is built to comply with Google Play's Families Policy, the United States Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including provisions concerning children. Because the app collects no data at all, there is no children's data to protect, disclose, sell or delete.

11. Your rights

Data protection law gives you rights of access, rectification and erasure over your personal data. As we hold no data about any user, there is nothing for us to provide, correct or delete. You may still contact us with any question at the address below.

12. Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app introduces any data collection — for example if advertising is added — this policy will be updated before that version is published, and the "last updated" date above will change. We encourage you to review this page after app updates.

13. Contact

Questions about this policy: dev@farahland.app
